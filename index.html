Cyber Safety Guide

Your complete guide to staying safe on the internet

Protect Personal Information

  • Never share passwords, address, phone number, or school info.
  • Use strong passwords & avoid posting private details online.
  • Enable 2FA everywhere you can.

Be Careful With Strangers

  • Avoid responding to unknown messages.
  • Do not click unknown links sent by strangers.
  • If someone behaves badly → Block + Report.

Think Before You Click

  • Beware of emails saying you won a prize.
  • Check the URL before entering personal information.
  • Do not download files from untrusted websites.

Protect Your Devices

  • Install antivirus software.
  • Keep your device updated.
  • Use lock screen security (PIN, password, fingerprint).

Stay Safe on Social Media

  • Keep your account private.
  • Don’t accept friend requests from strangers.
  • Never share your live location.

Gaming Safety

  • Do not share personal info in game chats.
  • Avoid scam websites offering free skins or diamonds.
  • Report toxic or harmful players.

Recognize Cyber Threats

  • Phishing (fake emails & messages)
  • Malware & viruses
  • Identity theft
  • Cyberbullying

What to Do If Something Happens?

  • Tell a trusted adult, teacher, or parent.
  • Change your passwords immediately.
  • Run a virus scan.
  • Block and report the attacker.
